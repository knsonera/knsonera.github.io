Platformer Game

Greedy Princess

A bright side-scrolling platform game where you collect crystals, avoid danger, and push your score higher with every run.

Play Now

Why Players Keep Coming Back

Greedy Princess combines approachable controls with layered challenge. You can enjoy it casually or chase cleaner, faster, and higher-scoring clears.

What You Can Do

  • Play through 6 handcrafted levels, including bonus stages that raise the challenge.
  • Collect crystals to unlock exits and hunt harder bonus routes for bigger rewards.
  • Face multiple enemy styles and use timing-based movement to survive and counter.
  • Use checkpoints and persistent profile stats to improve your runs over time.
  • Choose your challenge level with easy, normal, and hard difficulty modes.

Best For

Players who enjoy colorful platform adventures with satisfying progression and score-chasing replay value.
