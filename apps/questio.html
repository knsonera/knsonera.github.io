Habit App

QuestiO: Habit Quests

QuestiO turns habit-building into a shared quest. Instead of trying to stay consistent alone, you build momentum with friends.

Download on iOS Join Android Waitlist

Why It Works

Consistency is easier when your progress is visible and social. QuestiO gives your habits a clear structure, a team dynamic, and a reason to keep showing up.

What You Can Do

  • Create quests for any habit, from fitness and reading to mindfulness and self-care.
  • Invite friends, track each other, and stay encouraged with social momentum.
  • Set realistic timeframes so habit goals feel doable and measurable.
  • Turn routine actions into a shared journey that feels rewarding, not rigid.

Best For

People who want accountability without pressure, and who stay motivated longer when growth feels collaborative and game-like.
