Our Mission

At Mind Games Lab, our mission is to combine cutting-edge technology, gamification, and psychological insights to help people build better habits, foster meaningful connections, and gain deeper self-awareness. We create innovative, engaging tools that make personal growth enjoyable and accessible for everyone.

Projects

Habit Buddies

Habit Buddies is a community-driven app designed to make building new habits engaging and collaborative. It combines smart habit-tracking tools with the power of social connections, enabling you to grow alongside your friends. Share progress, motivate each other, and celebrate milestones together, all while forming lasting positive habits.

Currently in development.

Arcana App

The Arcana App uses tarot cards as a psychological tool to promote self-reflection and personal discovery. It offers thought-provoking insights that encourage users to explore their inner world. It provides a fun and entertaining way to tackle daily challenges and find inspiration for new beginnings.

Currently in development.

Volunteer Work

Gurudevi Nirmalananda App

Developed for the spiritual teacher Gurudevi Nirmalananda Saraswati, a modern-day sage and Guru, this app provides yogic wisdom for modern living. The app contains a collection of quotes, teachings, meditative insights, podcasts and videos, and informs the yogic community about upcoming programs and events.

Available on App Store and Google Play.
