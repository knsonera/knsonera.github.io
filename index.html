Habit Buddies

HabitBuddies is a mobile app that turns habit-building into a team adventure. If you want to start running, reading, meditating, or even flossing regularly, you can create a quest in HabitBuddies for a week, a month, or any timeframe and invite friends to build the habit together.

It’s easier together—you can discuss progress and achievements in a chat, support each other with likes and power-ups, and track your friends’ progress. This makes the process not only effective but also genuinely fun!

Currently in development.

Arcana App

Arcana App is a simple and inspiring way to see any situation from a new perspective using tarot cards—without mysticism or magic. The app offers a variety of spreads for all kinds of questions, plus a Card of the Day feature to bring you guidance, support, or a spark of inspiration whenever you need it.

Your readings are saved locally, and if you ever want a deeper interpretation, you can send your cards to a professional tarot reader for a detailed consultation.

Currently in development.

Gurudevi Nirmalananda App

Developed for the spiritual teacher Gurudevi Nirmalananda Saraswati, a modern-day sage and Guru, this app provides yogic wisdom for modern living. The app contains a collection of quotes, teachings, meditative insights, podcasts and videos, and informs the yogic community about upcoming programs and events.

Available on App Store and Google Play.
