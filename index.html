Habit Buddies

HabitBuddies is a mobile app that turns habit-building into a team adventure. If you want to start running, reading, meditating, or even flossing regularly, you can create a quest in HabitBuddies for a week, a month, or any timeframe and invite friends to build the habit together.

It’s easier together—you can discuss progress and achievements in a chat, support each other with likes and power-ups, and track your friends’ progress. This makes the process not only effective but also genuinely fun!

Currently in development.

Don't You Dare

Don’t You Dare is a playful self-challenge app that turns everyday life into a bold little adventure. Each day, you get a fun, doable dare that nudges you to step outside your comfort zone, from acts of confidence and creativity to bursts of joy and self-care.

No pressure, no regrets, these lighthearted dares will make you feel more alive, one day at a time. Celebrate your wins, reroll if you’re not feeling it, and rediscover how good it feels to be brave.

Beta testing on TestFlight (iOS) and Google Play (Android).

Arcana

Arcana is a fun and simple self-discovery tool, a web app that helps you look inward without taking it all too seriously. It’s not about predicting the future, it’s about pausing, reflecting, and seeing everyday decisions from a new angle.

Inspired by traditional tarot imagery, Arcana turns self-reflection into a small daily ritual of curiosity and encouragement. Think of it as a conversation starter with yourself, less about destiny, more about discovering what’s already within you.

Currently in development.

Gurudevi Nirmalananda App

Developed for the spiritual teacher Gurudevi Nirmalananda Saraswati, a modern-day sage and Guru, this app provides yogic wisdom for modern living. The app contains a collection of quotes, teachings, meditative insights, podcasts and videos, and informs the yogic community about upcoming programs and events.

Available on App Store and Google Play.
